Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and store information.

Overview

We only collect information that helps us run the website and respond to enquiries.

What we collect

  • Information you submit via forms (e.g. name, email, message).
  • Basic usage data (e.g. pages visited, browser type) via standard analytics, if enabled.

How we use it

  • To respond to enquiries.
  • To improve the website and content.
  • To meet legal obligations.

Cookies

This website uses cookies to ensure it functions correctly. We do not use advertising or marketing cookies.

Essential cookies

Essential cookies are required for basic site functionality. These include remembering your cookie preferences and enabling core features such as navigation and security. These cookies are always enabled and do not store personally identifiable information.

Analytics cookies

Analytics cookies help us understand how the site is used so we can improve clarity and usefulness. These cookies are optional and are only set if you explicitly accept them.

At present, we do not run analytics or tracking software. If this changes, analytics cookies will only be enabled after consent is given via the cookie banner.

Your choices

When you first visit this site, you are asked whether to accept analytics cookies. You can choose to accept or reject them.

If you wish to change your decision later, you can do so by clearing this site’s data in your browser and reloading the page.

How long we keep it

We keep data only as long as necessary for the purposes above, or as required by law.

Your rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data where applicable.

Contact

If you have questions, contact us using the details on the website.
